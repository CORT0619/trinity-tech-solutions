About Us

Who we are.

Trinity Tech Solutions, LLC was founded in October of 2023 by Crystal Williams. Trinity Tech Solutions is a remote small business, operating primarily in the Central Florida area, that provides technical solutions to small businesses. The goal of Trinity Tech Solutions is to support small businesses by helping to offload the mundane technical tasks so that clients are able to focus on what they enjoy and do best.

While Trinity Tech offers a wide variety of services such as social media assistance and assistance with word processing tasks, our primary focus is in software troubleshooting and website management. At Trinity Tech Solutions are mission is to help take small businesses to the next level by helping them to focus on their core functions and to provide them peace of mind knowing that they have hired a reliable company who is invested in its clients. Our vision is to create small businesses who are able to operate seemlessly knowing that they have the tools needed to not only get the job done but to innovate, excel and be empowered.
